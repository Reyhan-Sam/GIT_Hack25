About

Secure

We do not store the file in any part of our data. This is an extremely essential thing to note as that worries a lot of our audience. We want to ensure a safe and easy way to send your data in and have it easily removed. This will be working through our machine learning model, not by us.

Potential Red Flags

Red Flags can be damaging in so many different ways and we need to be more aware of them. This resource helps with that so you can see how these things could affect your life, leading to hopefully better decisions than automatically clicking the accept button.

Yellow Flags

What this will also have are yellow flags - Items that can cause concern to the reader but not guaranteed. These could create problems in the future but it is not certain, so it depends on your opinion about the circumstance. We want you to have all the information you need.

Green Flags

Even with all tese flags, we want you to have the best outcome and make the best decision you can. Thus, FinePrint will also help in indicating the positives (hence, the green flags) of each company, so you get to weigh the positives and the negatives. The decision is up to you!

Use 1

Our AI model uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) to analyse the uploaded document sentence by sentence, provide a summary, and categorise information into the three different categories that were mentioned above: Red Flag, Yellow Flag, and Green Flag.

Use 2

The AI model was trained manually, using test data which consisted of sentences containing common clauses and keywords found in contracts; using sentences from each of our categories, we trained the model to accurately recognise and classify sentences.

Use 3

Our product also provides a detailed summary and analysis of the uploaded document, detailing key points and clauses outlined in the contract. It uses easy-to-understand language in order to ensure our user is able to fully understand exactly what they are agreeing to. The format of our output is also simple and user friendly, ensuring our users have an efficient and seamless experience.
